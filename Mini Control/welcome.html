Welcome to Mini Control Extension!

Thank you for installing the Mini Control Extension for Chrome! This extension is designed to give you full control over media playback in your browser, allowing you to manage videos and music effortlessly. Whether you're watching videos on YouTube, streaming music, or enjoying any other form of media, this extension makes it easier to control your experience.

Features

  • Play/Pause Control: Easily play or pause any media content with a single click.
  • Volume Adjustment: Adjust the volume using a convenient slider, with real-time percentage display.
  • Playback Speed Control: Change the playback speed of videos and music. Choose from a range of speeds from slow to fast.
  • Mute and Loop: Mute audio with a click and enable looping for continuous playback.
  • Customizable Theme: Switch between light and dark modes to suit your preference and reduce eye strain.
